What Is Suicide Cleanup?

Suicide cleanup is the professional process of remediating spaces affected by a suicide. It requires specialized training, protective equipment, and technical expertise to ensure safety, compliance, and discretion throughout the process.

  • Biohazard Removal: Safe removal of all bloodborne pathogens and biological materials
  • Complete Sanitization: Comprehensive cleaning and disinfection of all affected surfaces
  • Odor Remediation: Neutralizing odors at their molecular source—not just masking them
  • Discreet Service: Protecting your privacy with compassion and professionalism

Our technicians are highly trained to manage these sensitive scenes with technical precision and empathy, allowing families to focus on healing while we restore the affected space.

Why Professional Cleanup Is Essential

In the aftermath of a suicide, cleanup should never fall on loved ones. Professional remediation is essential for both safety and peace of mind.

  • Health Hazards: Exposure to biological materials poses serious health risks, even when contaminants are not visible.
  • Improper Sanitization: Household cleaning products cannot effectively decontaminate biohazard-affected areas. We use hospital-grade, EPA-approved solutions to ensure full remediation.
  • Emotional Trauma: Attempting to clean such scenes can deeply worsen grief and psychological distress.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Our team follows all OSHA, EPA, and state regulations for proper disposal of biohazardous waste.
