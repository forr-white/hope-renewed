Our Decomposition Cleanup Process

At Hope Renewed, we understand that decomposition scenes can be emotionally overwhelming and hazardous to handle alone. Our certified team follows a science-based, compassionate process to ensure complete remediation while minimizing disruption to you and your loved ones.

Immediate Response

Our 24/7 response team is ready to act at a moment’s notice. We typically arrive within hours of your call to contain the situation quickly and prevent further contamination or damage.

Comprehensive Assessment

Every situation is unique. Our technicians perform a meticulous inspection to identify all impacted areas—including those where biological contaminants may not be immediately visible.

Secure Containment

To safeguard the rest of your home or property, we establish secure containment zones. This prevents cross-contamination and protects unaffected areas during the cleanup process.

Biohazard Removal

Equipped with full protective gear, our specialists remove and dispose of all biological materials safely and discreetly, adhering strictly to OSHA, EPA, and state regulations.

Advanced Decontamination

We use medical-grade disinfectants, ATP-tested cleaning agents, and advanced tools to ensure every surface is properly sanitized—even in hard-to-reach areas.

Molecular Odor Elimination

Decomposition odors are treated at their source, not masked. We apply enzymatic and ozone-based odor neutralization techniques to fully eliminate unpleasant smells at the molecular level.

Structural Remediation

If contamination has affected building materials, we remove or restore damaged areas, ensuring your property’s integrity and safety are fully restored.

Final Verification & Restoration

Our final step includes ATP surface testing to verify a hospital-level standard of cleanliness. Once confirmed, we restore the space to a safe, habitable condition—free from visible and invisible traces of the incident.
